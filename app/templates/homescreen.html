Welcome Admin!

This is your tool for managing your policies for IoT-Devices in your Smart Home or Smart Building!

You have many different possibilites on how to interact with your policies.


You can look at all available policies in your building under the "Policies"-tab and share or delete them.

Under "New Policies" you can create your own policies.


The "Notifications"-tab shows all notifications that occur in your system. "Pending Policies" shows the policies you created, where you can decide if you want to add it to your system.


Finally in the "Community"-tab, you can download and add policies from other users to your system.
